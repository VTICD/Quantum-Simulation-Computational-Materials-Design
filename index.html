Introduction

VTICD 2025 brings together researchers, students, and professionals to advance quantum simulation methods and computational materials design. Jointly organized by National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (Taiwan, R.O.C.) and Hanoi National University of Education (Viet Nam), the workshop features expert-guided lectures, hands-on tutorials, and collaborative discussions. This edition also highlights WannierBSE, a practical workflow for excitonic and optical-spectrum calculations based on Wannier-function interpolation and the Bethe-Salpeter Equation, with a live demonstration and Q&A.
